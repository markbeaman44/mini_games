🪞 How to Play

🦸 Your Mission: Save all civilians using your hero and mirror reflections!

🪞 Mirror Reflections: Your hero in the center controls reflections in surrounding mirrors.

⚡ Synchronized Movement: All reflections move together with your main hero!

🎯 Strategy: Position reflections to reach civilians while avoiding obstacles.

💥 Danger: Reflections break if they hit obstacles! Protect them carefully.

🔄 Controls: Use WASD or arrow keys to move, or touch controls on mobile.
